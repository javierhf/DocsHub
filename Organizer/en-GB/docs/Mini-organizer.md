**MINIORGANIZER**  
  
**Mini-organizer** appears on the rigth side of the Organizer _after clicking on an order. We will find there all the **jobs** of an order _organized by day and mechanic_.  

![Miniorganizador](images/en-EN_organizer_miniorganizer.png)    

**Mini-organizer** visualize the following information:   
 
> - See extended information about the jobs of an order (mouse-over an order).    
> - Plan a job of the order to a different day and mechanic.  
< - ![](Images/en-EN_organizer_icons_moreoptions.png) 
Modify the time planned.
